Hey, I'm Sarthak Goel

And this is my tech journey...

I've always been fascinated by technology and its power to solve real-world problems. This passion led me to co-found Invoid, a venture that caught the eye of Y Combinator (W21 batch). We revolutionized identity verification for Indian enterprises, and yeah, it was quite a ride that ended with a successful acquisition!

But my journey didn't start or end there. Before and after my startup exit, I've been deeply involved with multiple startups, contributing to technology development and innovation. Each experience has taught me something new about building products that people love and scaling tech solutions.
