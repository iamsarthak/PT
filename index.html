About Me

I'm Sarthak Goel, a tech entrepreneur with a knack for solving real-world problems using innovative technology solutions. My entrepreneurial journey began with co-founding inVOID at the age of 22. Our mission at inVOID was to automate identity verification workflows for enterprises across India, simplifying the way businesses onboard customers while adhering to compliance requirements.

Our venture caught the eye of Y Combinator, one of the most prestigious startup accelerators, which provided us with invaluable support and resources to propel our venture forward. The journey with inVOID was a remarkable one, filled with learning, growth, and eventually a successful exit as our venture got acquired.

With a team of 15 dedicated individuals based in Gurugram, Haryana, India, we were able to create impactful products and technologies for fintechs and neo banks, making a significant mark in the Indian market.

Beyond inVOID, I've also been actively involved in the startup ecosystem, mentoring upcoming entrepreneurs and investing in promising ventures. I believe in the power of technology to drive change and am continually exploring new avenues to leverage tech for societal betterment.
